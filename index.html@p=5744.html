Privacy Policy

At Aatulya Wellness, we respect your privacy and are committed to safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the data you provide when interacting with our website and services. By using our platform, you agree to the practices outlined in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information from you in a variety of ways, including when you make bookings, register for events, or contact us directly. The types of personal data we may collect include:

  • Personal Identification Information: Name, email address, phone number, and payment details.
  • Usage Information: Information about your interactions with our website, such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited.

We may also collect information through cookies and other tracking technologies to enhance your experience on our site.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to provide and improve our services. Specifically, we may use your information to:

  • Process bookings, subscriptions, and other requests.
  • Communicate with you about your transactions and inquiries.
  • Improve and personalize your experience on our website.
  • Send you updates about upcoming events, promotions, or wellness programs.

We only use your data for purposes directly related to the services we offer.

3. Marketing Communications

From time to time, we may send you promotional offers or information about upcoming events. You can opt out of receiving such marketing communications at any time by:

  • Clicking the “unsubscribe” link in any email we send.
  • Contact us directly at aatulyayoga@gmail.com to request removal from our mailing list.

4. Data Protection

We take the security of your personal information seriously and have implemented appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect it. These measures include:

  • Encryption: We encrypt sensitive data, such as payment information, to safeguard it during transactions.
  • Secure Servers: Our servers are equipped with robust security protocols.
  • Regular Audits: We conduct regular security audits to assess and improve our data protection practices.

However, please remember that no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

To provide you with a seamless and personalized experience, we use cookies and similar technologies to collect information about how you interact with our site. This includes:

  • Session Cookies: Temporary cookies that help us manage your visit and remember your preferences during your session.
  • Persistent Cookies: Cookies that remain on your device after you leave the site to enhance future visits.
  • Third-Party Cookies: Cookies from trusted third-party services that help us analyze user behaviour and improve our services.

You can manage cookie settings and preferences through your browser settings, including disabling cookies altogether if preferred. However, some features of the site may not function properly without cookies.

6. Sharing Data with Third Parties

We may share your data with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in delivering our services, such as:

  • Payment processors for booking and transaction handling.
  • Event organizers for coordinating workshops and retreats.

These third-party providers are contractually obligated to keep your information confidential and to use it only for the purpose for which it was provided. We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

7. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to external sites that are not operated by Aatulya Wellness. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of these third-party websites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these sites before engaging with them.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information that we hold about you.
  • Update or correct any inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Delete your personal information from our records.
  • Opt out of receiving marketing communications at any time.

If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at aatulyayoga@gmail.com.

9. How to Access or Manage Your Information

You can access your account information or request updates or deletions by logging into your account on our platform. If you encounter any issues, feel free to reach out to us directly for assistance.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

Aatulya Wellness reserves the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated “Effective Date.” We recommend reviewing this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your personal information.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy or how we handle your personal information, please don’t hesitate to contact us at:
